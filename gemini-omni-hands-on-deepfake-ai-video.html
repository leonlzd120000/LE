Google's new anything-to-anything AI model is wild

Google 新的“万物转万物”AI 模型很疯狂

Gemini's Omni Flash model can generate AI video from real images and clips. Making it look like you're on a flight to France just got way easier.

来源 · The Verge 原文链接

Last year I deepfaked my kid's stuffed animal to make it look like his plush deer was on vacation.

去年，我用深度伪造技术处理了我孩子的毛绒玩具，让他的毛绒小鹿看起来像是在度假。

It was an experiment to see if I could re-create the events depicted in a Gemini ad Google was running, and I never showed the videos of Buddy the deer on his adventures to my four-year-old. But it was a revealing exercise that made me think a lot about the difference between some harmless fun with generative AI and full-on slop. Maybe that Venn diagram is a perfect circle! Maybe not. But what I know for sure is that the tools to make realistic videos are surprisingly good, requiring surprisingly little effort and know-how. And that trend is continuing hot into Gemini's Omni era.

这是一次实验，看看我能不能重现 Google 当时 Gemini 广告里展示的情节；我也从来没有把小鹿 Buddy 冒险的视频给我四岁的孩子看。但这次实验很有启发性，让我反复思考：用生成式 AI 做一点无伤大雅的玩笑，和制造彻底的 AI 垃圾内容之间，到底有什么区别。也许这个维恩图就是一个完美的圆，也许不是。但我可以确定的是，制作逼真视频的工具已经出人意料地好用，需要的努力和专业知识也少得惊人。而这个趋势正在 Gemini 的 Omni 时代继续升温。

Omni is a new family of generative models that will allegedly one day be able to turn any kind of input -- photo, video, text -- into anything else. But for starters, it's just creating video. Omni Flash is the first of these models Google has released, now available in the company's AI video generation and editing platform, Flow. You can still use the previous model, Veo, if you want, but Omni improves on Veo in a few ways.

Omni 是一组新的生成式模型，据称未来有一天能够把任何形式的输入--照片、视频、文本--转换成任何其他形式。不过一开始，它主要还是用来生成视频。Omni Flash 是 Google 发布的该系列首个模型，目前已经可以在 Google 的 AI 视频生成与编辑平台 Flow 中使用。如果你愿意，仍然可以使用之前的 Veo 模型，但 Omni 在几个方面比 Veo 有所改进。

With Omni, you can upload a video and use that along with a text prompt as the starting point for your AI-generated creation. Google also claims Omni incorporates more real-world knowledge when producing videos and can do a better job of keeping characters consistent throughout a video as a result. There was only one way to really know if those claims are true: I brought back AI Buddy to pack his little AI-generated bags for another adventure.

使用 Omni 时，你可以上传一段视频，并把它和文本提示一起作为 AI 生成创作的起点。Google 还声称，Omni 在生成视频时融入了更多现实世界知识，因此能更好地在整段视频中保持角色一致。要验证这些说法是否属实，只有一个办法：我把 AI Buddy 又带了回来，让它收拾 AI 生成的小行李，准备再次冒险。

The results are such a mixed bag they're baffling. Some were very good -- much more consistent and true to my prompt than when I was testing out Veo five months ago. But even the best clips Omni cooked up for me still have certain AI jump scares, like when Buddy suddenly switches orientation while he's skydiving.

结果好坏参半，甚至令人困惑。有些结果非常好，比我五个月前测试 Veo 时更稳定，也更贴合我的提示词。但即使是 Omni 为我生成的最佳片段，仍然会出现某些 AI 式的突兀惊吓，比如 Buddy 在跳伞时突然改变朝向。

For another video, I gave Omni some artistic freedom. Create a montage of Buddy packing for a vacation and embarking on a cruise ship for a tropical vacation. The mood is cute and playful. Buddy packs something funny in his suitcase that comes into play later in the clip. It had Buddy pack a jar of honey; later in the clip he reaches for it as if it's a bottle of sunscreen.

在另一段视频里，我给了 Omni 一些艺术自由。我提示它制作一个蒙太奇，内容是 Buddy 为度假打包，并登上一艘邮轮去热带度假。氛围要可爱、俏皮。于是它让 Buddy 打包了一罐蜂蜜；后来 Buddy 把它当成防晒霜一样拿起来。

Honestly, not a bad bit. Except that the bottle of honey constantly changes throughout the video, from a jar, to a clear squirt bottle filled with water, then back to a squeeze bottle filled with honey. And I can't even begin to describe how the model came up with the final frame of the video.

老实说，这个桥段还不错。问题在于，那瓶蜂蜜在整段视频里不断变形：一会儿是罐子，一会儿变成装着水的透明挤压瓶，随后又变回装满蜂蜜的挤压瓶。至于模型是怎么生成最后一帧的，我甚至不知道该如何描述。

You can use text-based prompts to suggest edits to your videos, and I'll give Google credit: This works better with Omni than it did when I tested Veo 3. But the results were bad with Veo. Omni will actually take your edits on board, but the results don't always hit.

你可以用基于文本的提示来建议修改视频，这一点我得给 Google 认可：Omni 在这方面比我测试 Veo 3 时表现更好。Veo 的修改结果很糟。Omni 确实会吸收你的修改意见，但结果并不总是到位。

I had it emphasize Buddy's facial reactions in his vacation clips, and the results just wound up looking strange. It would also give Buddy antlers from time to time, which he does not have. When I prompted it to remove the antlers that appeared in one scene, it obliged -- and then added antlers in all the other ones.

我让它强化 Buddy 在度假片段中的面部反应，结果看起来只是更奇怪。它还会时不时给 Buddy 加上鹿角，但 Buddy 本来没有鹿角。当我提示它移除某个场景里出现的鹿角时，它照做了--然后又在其他所有场景里加上了鹿角。

The thing is, none of this is free. Generating videos costs credits, varying from 15 to 40 credits based on the length of the scene and the ingredients you start with. One round of edits costs 40 credits.

问题是，这一切都不是免费的。生成视频需要消耗点数，根据场景长度和你提供的素材不同，每次会消耗 15 到 40 点。一轮编辑就要 40 点。

One of Omni's purported strengths is adding AI-generated stuff to real videos, so I gave Buddy a break and deepfaked myself. Starting with a selfie video with a neutral expression, I prompted Omni to generate videos of me eating a plate of spaghetti, sitting in an airplane seat, and standing in front of the Eiffel Tower taking a bite out of a baguette.

Omni 据称的一大强项，是把 AI 生成的内容添加到真实视频里，所以我让 Buddy 休息一下，转而深度伪造了我自己。我从一段表情中性的自拍视频开始，提示 Omni 生成我吃意大利面、坐在飞机座位上，以及站在埃菲尔铁塔前咬一口法棍的视频。

There are AI tells in my deepfake videos. The clink of the fork hitting the bowl of pasta is a little too manufactured. There's a woman in the background of the airplane video who shows up twice. But aside from those little glitches and a vaguely uncanny sense about them, they're convincing as hell.

我的深度伪造视频里确实有 AI 痕迹。叉子碰到意大利面碗的叮当声显得有点太刻意。飞机视频背景里还有一个女人出现了两次。但除了这些小瑕疵，以及一种隐约的诡异感之外，它们真的非常有说服力。

I showed my husband the pasta clip; he knew I was testing an AI video tool but I didn't tell him what in the scene had been generated by AI. The pasta-eating itself looked real enough to convince my husband.

我把吃意大利面的片段给我丈夫看；他知道我在测试 AI 视频工具，但我没有告诉他画面中哪些部分是 AI 生成的。吃面的动作本身真实到足以骗过我丈夫。

My other deepfakes are varying levels of good enough to fool people on social media. A couple of the Eiffel Tower clips look slightly cartoonish, but one of them is convincing enough that you might need to rewatch it a few times to clock that it's AI.

我的其他深度伪造视频则处于不同程度的足以骗过社交媒体用户水平。有几个埃菲尔铁塔片段看起来略带卡通感，但其中一个足够逼真，你可能需要反复看几遍才会意识到它是 AI。

I'm a little exhausted by it all, to be honest. I was shocked when I tested Veo 3 at the realism it could produce. I should probably be shocked by Omni too, and I guess I am, but the edge has worn off.

坦白说，我对这一切有点疲惫了。我测试 Veo 3 时，曾被它能产生的真实感震惊。按理说我也应该被 Omni 震惊，我想我的确有些震惊，但那种锋利的冲击感已经磨损了。

It's still not quite as easy to make an AI-generated cinematic masterpiece as Google would like you to believe. But Omni does improve on Veo in some recognizable ways. I don't think we're at the foothills of the singularity exactly, but we're definitely deep in the uncanny valley.

要制作一部 AI 生成的电影级杰作，仍然没有 Google 想让你相信的那么容易。但 Omni 确实在一些明显方面改进了 Veo。我不认为我们已经处在所谓奇点山脚下，但我们肯定已经深入了恐怖谷。
